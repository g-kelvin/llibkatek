About our company

Llibkatec Limited is a solution provider for customers who want to communicate and connect with consumers. We specialize in outdoor advertising, Corporate branding and general printing.
We integrate product position and personality to make the end product a more valuable asset to your Client. Llibkatec Limited was incorporated with the principle objective of meeting the increasing demands of clients throught out the country for high quality innovative communication services, provision of solutions around visibility and consistency with competitive costs.
Within our operation is a creative design department that articulate design concepts that work work best with respective corporate advertising and promotional strategies.
With our unique designs, we enhance a wide variety of corporate theme and messages that portrays your identity.


Our Vision
To provide high resolution prints with consistent quality at competitive cost to our client Our Mission
To be a leader in providing innovative visibility media solutions to our defined markets.
